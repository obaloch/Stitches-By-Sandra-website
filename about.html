Founded May 2021

My name is Sandra, I have been a resident of Bloomington IL for 23 years, and I have decided to test my entrepreneurial side since 2021, by incorporate one of my favorite hobbies, into my business.

I am at the local Farmer's Market once a month, which allowes me to interact with people that are interested in my products, that are also available personalized. I am open to new ideas or suggestions about what people like, because everybody love unique high quality items that have been made just for them.

I have always been amazed of how people can transform "simple materials" into beautiful art pieces. I believe when you put your heart, soul, passion, and love into something, the result will always be art through your hands.

Since 2021 I started a journey completely new to me, and even though it has been challenging, when I see an smile on people's face when they see my creations, I know everything is worth it.
